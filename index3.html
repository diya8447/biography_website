Biography

“Believe in yourself and make your dreams a reality.”

Kendall Jenner(born November 3, 1995) is an American model, television personality, and businesswoman. She gained fame as part of the Kardashian-Jenner family, appearing on the reality TV show *Keeping Up with the Kardashians* from 2007 to 2021. Born in Los Angeles, California, she is the daughter of Kris Jenner and Caitlyn Jenner (formerly Bruce Jenner). Kendall began her modeling career at age 14 and rose to prominence in the fashion industry through high-profile campaigns and runway shows for brands like Chanel, Versace, and Givenchy. She became one of the world's highest-paid models, gracing the covers of major magazines such as *Vogue* and *Harper's Bazaar*. In addition to modeling, Kendall has launched successful business ventures, including her tequila brand, 818. Known for her minimalist and chic style, she has carved a unique niche for herself in the celebrity and fashion world, balancing her fame with a relatively private personal life compared to her siblings.

Kendall Jenner is a prominent figure in the fashion world, celebrated for her elegance and versatility. On the runway, she embodies the vision of high-end designers, walking for iconic brands like Chanel, Versace, and Marc Jacobs. Her presence at major fashion weeks in New York, Paris, Milan, and London has cemented her reputation as one of the most sought-after supermodels of her generation. Known for her ability to adapt to various styles, she seamlessly transitions between avant-garde couture and ready-to-wear collections. Off the runway, Kendall's fashion sense is the epitome of effortless chic. Her street style is a masterclass in modern minimalism, favoring clean lines, neutral tones, and tailored silhouettes. She often pairs wardrobe staples like oversized blazers, crop tops, and denim with bold accessories, creating looks that are both timeless and trendsetting. On the red carpet, Kendall is known for her daring choices, frequently wearing gowns with sheer fabrics, intricate embellishments, and dramatic cutouts that highlight her statuesque figure.
